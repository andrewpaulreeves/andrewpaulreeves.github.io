Soapy

Simulation Optique Adaptative - in Python

Soapy is an intuitive and powerful Adaptive Optics (AO) simulation. It has bene designed from the ground up to enable rapid development of new ideas for AO. This is facilitated partly by being writted entirely in the Python programming language.
